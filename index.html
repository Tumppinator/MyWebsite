Welcome to my personal website! Ever since I first learned about the Oculus Kickstarter in 2012, my passion for XR (extended reality) technologies has been unwavering. I am fascinated by the possibilities that virtual reality offers and love exploring them through programming projects and experiments.
When I'm not immersed in the world of XR, you can find me indulging in my hobbies, such as improvising on the piano, creating custom levels for Beat Saber, and exploring various VR painting apps. I'm always eager to try new experiences, whether it's riding the waves while surfing or honing my dance skills through improvisation lessons.
On top of my technological pursuits, I have a remarkable athletic background as a former javelin thrower who competed at both national and international levels. One of the most memorable moments in my javelin throwing career was participating in the inaugural Youth Olympic Games held in Singapore in 2010.
